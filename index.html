🚨 HYPOGLYCEMIA PROTOCOL
15-15 Rule
Step 1 — Eat 15g fast carbs NOW
4 glucose tabs · 4oz (120ml) juice · 1 tbsp honey · 1 tube glucose gel · 4oz regular soda
Step 2 — Wait 15 minutes, then recheck BG
If still <70 mg/dL, repeat Step 1. Once >70, eat a small snack with protein + slow carbs (crackers + peanut butter, cheese + crackers).
Call 911 / Emergency if unconscious or unable to swallow
988 — Crisis line  |  Glucagon / Baqsimi — use if available
